How we keep you safe
AUTOPILOT
Collins Pro Line Fusion®
The autopilot bar you see at the top — LNAV, VNAV, A/P — is managed by Collins. It processes 3,200 flight parameters per second so the pilot can focus on decision-making.
99.97%dispatch reliability
SPEED & ALTITUDE
Air Data Systems
The speed and altitude tapes are fed by Collins Air Data Computers — measuring pitot pressure to 0.1 knot accuracy. Redundant triple systems ensure one failure never affects the pilot.
3×redundant sensors
NAVIGATION
Flight Management System
The heading tape and route are computed by the Collins FMS — combining GPS, inertial reference, and VOR data. Sub-10 metre accuracy anywhere on Earth, even over oceans with no radar coverage.
<10mposition accuracy
SITUATIONAL AWARENESS
TCAS II & Weather Radar
Collins TCAS II continuously scans surrounding airspace for traffic conflicts, issuing resolution advisories to the pilot. The weather radar ahead detects turbulence up to 320 nautical miles.
